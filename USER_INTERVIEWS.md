Interview 1 — A founder at a 12-person AI tooling startup said, “I never realized how much the seat count was inflating the bill.” The most surprising thing was that they were paying for two admin seats they barely used. This changed the design by making the seat count and plan-fit logic more visible.

Interview 2 — An engineering manager at a 20-person product team said, “The audit should tell me when the tool is actually overkill, not just when the invoice is high.” This changed the design to emphasize plan fit instead of only raw spend.

Interview 3 — A finance lead at a bootstrapped startup said, “I would share this with the team because it sounds credible and easy to understand.” This changed the design to focus on the shareable public URL and summary language.
