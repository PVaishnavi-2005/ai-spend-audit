# Metrics

North Star metric: completed audits with a shareable result page.

Three input metrics that drive the North Star:
1. Number of tool rows filled per session.
2. Average monthly savings shown to the user.
3. Share-link copy rate after the audit is generated.

What to instrument first:
- Audit completion rate
- Public share click-through rate
- Lead capture conversion rate from completed audits

Pivot trigger: if fewer than 10% of audit sessions become shareable public links, the product should pivot toward a more specific audience or a simpler pricing explanation.
